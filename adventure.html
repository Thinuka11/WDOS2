Embark on thrilling adventures right from the comfort of our hotel. Our carefully curated adventure packages offer a perfect blend of excitement and relaxation. Diving




Diving is a thrilling aquatic escapade that beckons the adventurous spirit. As you submerge into the cool, azure waters, a realm of wonder unfolds. The world beneath the waves is a symphony of colors and life â€“ vibrant coral gardens sway with the current, schools of fish dart gracefully, and hidden treasures like shipwrecks and mysterious caves await discovery. With every descent, you leave the terrestrial world behind, entering a realm of weightlessness and serenity. It's a chance to explore the mysteries of the deep, where encounters with marine creatures and the feeling of floating in liquid space create indelible memories. Whether you're snorkeling near the shore or plunging into the depths with scuba gear, diving promises an immersive experience like no other, where the beauty and tranquility of the underwater world come alive.

Duration: 4-6 Hours

Price: RS.10000 (One Person)

Jet Ski(Water Scooter)




Jet skiing is the epitome of high-speed aquatic thrill. Straddling a sleek machine, you rev the engine and set out onto the water. The wind rushes through your hair as you skim across the water's surface, defying gravity with every leap over waves. The world blurs around you as you unleash the full power of your watercraft, creating an exhilarating dance between man and machine. Whether you're racing with friends, exploring coastal gems, or simply enjoying the freedom of the open sea, jet skiing is a pulse-pounding adventure that offers an adrenaline rush like no other. It's a chance to embrace the sea's vast playground, where the only limit is your thirst for speed and excitement.

Duration: 1 Hour

Price: Rs.5000(2 adults)

Whale Watching




Embarking on a whale-watching excursion is like entering a realm of giants beneath the sea. As you venture into the open waters, your heart races with anticipation, knowing that at any moment, you might witness the breathtaking breach of a colossal whale. The salty breeze tangles your hair as you scan the horizon, eagerly awaiting the telltale spout or fin that signals their presence. These gentle behemoths surface with grace, casting an awe-inspiring spectacle before you. With every sighting, you're reminded of the immense beauty and mystery that the ocean holds, and you can't help but feel a profound connection to the planet's most magnificent creatures. Whale watching is not just an adventure; it's a humbling and soul-stirring encounter with the wonders of the deep.

Duration: 3-5 hours

Price: Rs.2500(One Person)


Deep-Sea Fishing



Deep sea fishing is an exhilarating plunge into the abyss, a journey to where the ocean's heart beats strongest. As you sail further from the shore, the waters grow deeper, and the anticipation builds. Here, amidst the boundless blue, you'll encounter some of the ocean's most formidable and enigmatic inhabitants. With the sun warming your back and the salty spray on your face, you cast your line into the depths, hoping for a tug that could be the catch of a lifetime. The battle that follows is a true test of strength and skill, as you engage in an epic struggle with creatures that have ruled the deep for eons. But it's not just about the thrill of the hunt; it's about being humbled by the vastness of the ocean and connecting with nature in its purest form. Deep sea fishing is a reminder that adventure awaits where the sea meets the unknown.


Duration: 4-6 Hours

Price: Rs.2500
